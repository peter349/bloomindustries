About us

Welcome to Bloom Industries

Established in the year 1988, we are becoming as one of the promising manufacturers and suppliers of quality 7 Watt LED Bulbs. They are the type of electric lamps that produce light with the help of light emitting diodes. These bulbs work on the principle of electroluminescence and find their application for both commercial and domestic sectors. Offered 7 Watt LED Bulbs have zero UV emissions, high durability, and great light dispersement.
